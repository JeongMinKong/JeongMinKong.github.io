Jeong Min Kong

I am an MASc Candidate in Electrical Engineering at the University of Toronto, working with Professor Elvino Sousa as a member of the Wireless Communications Group. I completed my BASc in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the same institute in 2022.

My current research interests broadly span mathematical game theory, information theory, and machine learning. Most recently, I worked on evaluating the performance of newly proposed deep generative model with Professor Adrian Nachman at the University of Toronto. Previously, I did research in reconfigurable intelligent surfaces under Professor Merouane Debbah and Professor George Alexandropoulos at Huawei Research, and in UAV communications under Professor Ekram Hossain at the University of Manitoba.
