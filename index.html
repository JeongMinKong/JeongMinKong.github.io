I am a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UCLA and an Amazon AI PhD Fellow. I received my BASc and MASc in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Toronto in 2022 and 2024, respectively. My primary research interest lies in machine learning, including its application to wireless communication and sensing.
