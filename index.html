I am a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UCLA. I received my BASc and MASc in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Toronto in 2022 and 2024, respectively. My current research interests span communication theory, information theory, and machine learning, including full-duplex systems, satellite communications, and distributed learning.
